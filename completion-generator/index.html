About this tool
Every unit at Harland ends in a measurable, the piece of work that proves a student finished the unit and shows their progress. This tool turns that measurable into a polished, on-brand completion document. Pick the measurable type, fill in the student details and the result or rubric, add any attachments, then write or generate the teacher comment. Use Download PDF to save and share the finished document.
