Admission Application

Thank you for considering Harland for your child. The assessment is a real lesson, taught 1-on-1 by a subject-specialist teacher, alongside an admission interview with a member of our academic leadership team. It is how we come to understand your child before we recommend anything. Completing this application reserves your child's place for the assessment.

Student and family details

About the assessment

Online diagnostic

Before the assessment, your child completes a short online diagnostic, about 45 minutes. It lets us prepare the right content for the assessment, so it must be completed more than 48 hours beforehand. If it is not completed in time, we will need to reschedule.

On the day

Your child needs no preparation, only to relax and show how they usually work. During the assessment you will have an admission interview with a member of our academic leadership team, to discuss your child's path together.
